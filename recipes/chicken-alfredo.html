Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Salt & pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • 1tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 2 servings fettucine pasta (or whatever you have)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (or garlic powder)

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
    • Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder
    • Cook in a pan with oil (medium heat) ~5-7 min per side
    • Set aside, then slice
  2. Boil Pasta
    • Salt your water
    • Cook pasta normally, save a little pasta water
  3. Make the sauce (Same pan)
    • Lower heat
    • Add a bit of butter or oil
    • Throw in garlic (30 seconds)
    • Pour in heavy cream
    • Let it simmer 2-3 minutes
  4. Add cheese
    • Slowly add parmesan whiles tirring
    • Sauce should thicken (if too thick, add pasta water
  5. Combine
    • Add pasta + chicken into the sauce
    • Mix everything together
  6. Enjoy!
